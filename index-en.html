WHAT WE DO

We incubate talents and accelerate projects to create opportunities for everyone.

We developed a disruptive model of open organization, where we create virtual environments that allow people to learn and practice different professional skills within a wide variety of specialities.

HOW WE DO IT

HOW WE WORK

WE ADAPT TO MODERN LIFE

The program is developed to adapt to the needs of the members, fostering innovation, creativity and productivity.

WE ARE THE PLATFORM TO SUCCESS

We focus on providing resources and networking to help people reach their full professional potential.

WE OPERATE WITH TRANSPARENCY

Any income generated through donations, grants or returns on projects is invested in the internships and the program to help more people in the future. We have publicly available balance sheets and metrics.


WE GENERATE JOB OPPORTUNITIES

We create ventures where they are most needed, generating jobs and products or services related to social impact.

WE TRAIN THE LEADERS OF TOMORROW

We facilitate the opportunity to develop a portfolio and a professional career in a friendly and collaborative environment. We implement an online learning platform that allows community members to learn and develop professional skills.

WE ARE CUTTING-EDGE

We use cutting-edge technology to offer an unique learning experience. This includes artificial intelligence, industry developments and trends, and the findings of professionals from all areas.

¿Questions?

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Inkua's participants are involved in projects related to a wide variety of disciplines. The proposals answers to their interests and professional needs, for which they count with the tools and processes that we provide.

Besides of gaining professional experience and applying new knowledge and tools, the participants will receive job references from InkuA, develop their personal portfolio with quality materials, gain autonomy managing their own schedules, develop their teamwork skills, and have the support and resources of other areas of InkuA to reach their goals.

The program is free, the collaborations and all the materials generated, are under the license GLP-3.0, which guarantees the permanent freedom for everyone. If the results are extraordinary or the program generates new opportunities, they will be rewarded, always and whenever they generate profits and positive results for the community and the participants.

The proposed program has a duration of six (6) months, during which the participants will be able to participate in a specific area of InkuA in interaction with other teams. At the end of the program, they will be able to renew their participation for six months more, either in the same area or in a different one.

The times are managed by the participants themselves, connecting according to their possibilities. We only require a minimum of 24 hours per month distributed throughout each week.

We always seek to collaborate and help people and organizations that work to make the world a better place, either through a product, a service or a message with positive social or ecological impact. We evaluate all projects that are sent to us and always provide feedback. We use a model of analysis impact that allows us to diagram the distribution of volunteers, funds and resources.

Somos una fundación sin fines de lucro de organización abierta con base en Frankfurt (Alemania). Nuestra misión es generar impacto social positivo en América Latina mediante la inclusión laboral y el desarrollo de proyectos y emprendimientos tecnológicos. Generamos prácticas donde se aplican herramientas tecnológicas y modelos de trabajo innovadores, y acompañamos a quienes necesitan adquirir experiencia laboral en distintas áreas para mejorar sus oportunidades de contratación. Buscamos potenciar y descubrir nuevos talentos e impulsar su crecimiento personal y profesional, ayudándolos a que mejoren sus habilidades e incrementen sus destrezas. We are an open non-profit organization based in Frankfurt (Germany). Our mission is to generate social impact in Latin America through employment and technology development. We generate practices where technology tools and innovative work models are applied, and we support those who need to acquire work experience in different areas to improve their opportunities for employment. We seek to empower and discover new talents and empower their personal and professional growth, helping them to improve their skills and increase their abilities.
